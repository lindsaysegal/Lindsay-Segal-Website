Crochet/Knit Projects

I enjoy to knit and crochet during my free time, I taught myself duirng COVID and have been crocheting ever since. My Great-Grandmother was an avid knitter and crocheter. She would always make all of the women in the family a winter hat for the holidays and we would take a family photo wearing our hats. My Grandma and Mother also know how to crochet and knit as well. I enjoy to create hats, just like my Great-Grandmother, and stuffed animals, tote bags and scrunchies.
